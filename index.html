Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Soundbuilder ("the App") is developed by Redbeak Dev. This policy explains how we handle your information.

DATA WE COLLECT

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app data (your saved presets and settings) is stored locally on your device only. We do not have any access to this data.

DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

Because all app data is stored locally on your device, we do not have access and cannot retain any of your data. Your data remains on the device for as long as the App is installed.

You can delete your data at any time by: Clearing the App's data through your device settings by navigating to: Settings, Apps, Soundbuilder, Storage, Clear Data (Android) Uninstalling the App, which removes all locally stored data from your device

Once deleted through either method, the data cannot be recovered.

IN-APP PURCHASES

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to their respective privacy policies: - Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ - Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

ANALYTICS

We do not use analytics or tracking services.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

CHANGES

We may update this policy. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any changes.
